My Top 5 Malang's Tourist Destinations

Kampung Warna-warni Jodipan

Jodipan Colorful Village is an alternative for family tourism in the middle of Malang City. As the name implies, the house to the hallway in this village has a lively color. Many Instagramable photo spots can be found in Jodipan Colorful Village. Starting from the umbrella hallway, colorful stairs to the glass bridge.

This tour had gone viral on social media. The Jodipan Colorful Village ticket is very pocket-friendly, only IDR 5,000 for the day. This tourist spot is located in Jodipan Village, about a 10-minute walk from Malang City Station.

Floratourism San Terra de Lafonte

Florawisata San Terra de Lafonte is one of the tourist destinations in Malang to wash your eyes. Because, this tourist spot offers a wide view of flower gardens and refreshes the eyes. There are 700 types of flowers that can be seen at Florawisata San Terra de Lafonte. In addition, visitors can also see

There are many Instagramable spots to capture moments with family. For example, miniature Korean, Dutch, and Italian buildings. The ticket price for this attraction is IDR 30,000. Florawisata San Terra de Lafonte is located on Truno Joyo Street, Jurangrejo, Pandesari, Pujon District, Malang Regency.

Malang City Square

Malang City Square can be a choice of Malang tourist attractions that can be visited with family. Visitors can sit back and relax around the square while enjoying the bustle of Malang City. There are a number of activities that can be done in Malang City Square. Starting from cycling, culinary tours, relaxing under trees, playing with children, and taking pictures.

Malang City Square can be accessed for free by the public. Visitors only need to pay for parking of IDR 2,000 for motorbikes and IDR 5,000 for cars.

Museum Angkut

Museum Angkut can be a family choice for educational tours. Various types of classic cars are exhibited in this museum which covers almost 4 hectares. The museum also exhibits a variety of historic cars, for example the vehicle used by the First President of the Republic of Indonesia Soekarno. Museum Angkut also displays the Presidential Helicopter which is a presidential helicopter.

Various facilities are also ready to pamper the tour with the family. Museum Angkut has facilities ranging from the Nusantara Floating Market, Buckingham Palace, Gangster Town, F1 Simulator, China Town, to Flight Simulator. The entrance ticket to Museum Angkut is IDR 110,000 per person, both weekdays and weekends. The location of this tourist attraction is on Jalan Canal Sultan Agung Number 2, Batu City.

Malang Night Paradise

Malang Night Paradise can be a night tour option at night and can be enjoyed with family. This tourist location offers the beauty of a sparkling LED light garden and a lantern garden. The eyes will be spoiled with various colors of lights. Visitors can enjoy the glittering lights-themed attractions with their families. Call it, Lantern Park which contains Dancing Water Fountain to Lighting Tunnel.

Malang Night Paradise also provides rides for all ages, namely Magic Journey 2, Roemah 147, Adventure Land, and the Ganesya Museum. Malang Night Paradise tickets are priced at IDR 55,000 on weekdays and IDR 65,000 on weekends.
